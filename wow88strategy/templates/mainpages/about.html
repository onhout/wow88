Who we are

Our team has been involved with the equities and futures markets since 1993. Over many years of testing, we have developed proprietary indicators using futures markets which are designed to perform in all market conditions. The inputs to our trading strategies originate from professional traders with proven, long-term expertise in trading futures and options on futures.

Risk management and preservation of capital are paramount to our trading methodology. We enforce strict risk parameters on our trading strategies in an attempt to limit our exposure to possible losses.

Traders and investors must also realize that past performance is not indicative of future results and trading futures does involve a substantial risk of loss. There are no assurances or guarantees that any trading system or strategy will result in profit.
